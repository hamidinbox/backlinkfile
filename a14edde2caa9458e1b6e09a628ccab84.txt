<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: verdana;">After working all day for weeks, surely we will feel bored with daily activities that do not stop, whether it is related to work or school and college. there's nothing wrong with taking a vacation and taking off the workload for a while. Well, for those of you who live in East Java, you don't need to go far outside Java or outside East Java for a vacation, in Pacitan there is also a beach that has a beautiful charm that can amaze you.
Pacitan besides being known for many tourist attractions related to caves also turns out to have many beaches that are no less interesting than other beaches in East Java.&nbsp;</span></span></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: medium;"></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-wktH70o5Tms/YT1_EsVyqLI/AAAAAAAAKgc/_LZsv_DBRVA_dme4hJDPhw3Ho9p7QTKCwCLcBGAsYHQ/s999/00.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="621" data-original-width="999" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-wktH70o5Tms/YT1_EsVyqLI/AAAAAAAAKgc/_LZsv_DBRVA_dme4hJDPhw3Ho9p7QTKCwCLcBGAsYHQ/s16000/00.jpg" /></a></span></div><span style="font-size: medium;"><br /><span style="font-family: verdana;"><br /></span></span><p></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: verdana;">Pancer Door Beach is a beach that is still part of the Pacitan Bay which is located in the easternmost part and is directly opposite the South Java Sea.

Although it is still in the same line with Teleng Ria Beach, the waves at Pancer Door Beach are bigger. This is what surfers often use to surf, showing their skills in conquering the waves. Therefore, Pancer Door Beach is also often used as a destination by surfers from within and outside the country.&nbsp;</span></span></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: verdana;"></span></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-bf68u7OaUko/YT1_L_YCkcI/AAAAAAAAKgg/OUHk76y1kqINbg0MQdGw6jAf9B_p-q7VgCLcBGAsYHQ/s640/2017-11-15%2B05.18.41%2B1.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="480" data-original-width="640" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-bf68u7OaUko/YT1_L_YCkcI/AAAAAAAAKgg/OUHk76y1kqINbg0MQdGw6jAf9B_p-q7VgCLcBGAsYHQ/s16000/2017-11-15%2B05.18.41%2B1.jpg" /></a></span></div><span style="font-size: medium;"><br />&nbsp;</span><p></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: verdana;">For visitors who like surfing, Pancer Door Beach is also ready to pamper with the waves. There are two types of waves here, namely beach break and river mouth break. With a sandy seabed, this beach is suitable for all surfers, including beginners and those who are learning. At low tide, surfers often get barrels. While the tide is the right time for beginners to have fun. May to October is the best time to surf at Pancer.

The location is very close to the center of Pacitan City, approximately 5-10 minutes using a motorized vehicle. The road conditions are very good and there is a very large parking lot. The retribution ticket to enter the Pancer Beach tourist area is only charged on weekends (Saturday &amp; Sunday) especially for tourists outside the Pacitan area.</span></span></p><p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: verdana;">&nbsp;</span></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-oDqJKXzDQTM/YT2AjA6YV7I/AAAAAAAAKgo/uga6o5bMYAMP9JQTbypuQamGSBIFmBZ3ACLcBGAsYHQ/s1200/Pantai-Pancer-pacitan-jawa-timur.jpg" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="900" data-original-width="1200" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-oDqJKXzDQTM/YT2AjA6YV7I/AAAAAAAAKgo/uga6o5bMYAMP9JQTbypuQamGSBIFmBZ3ACLcBGAsYHQ/s16000/Pantai-Pancer-pacitan-jawa-timur.jpg" /></a></span></div><span style="font-size: medium;"><br /> besides you can surf on the beach. Pancer Door beach also has a sunset view that is no less beautiful.
