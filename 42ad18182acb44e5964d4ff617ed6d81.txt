<h2>Market hours and trading sessions</h2>

.<h3>Understanding the Forex Market and Trading Sessions. .</h3>
The forex market is a decentralized global market where currencies are traded. It operates 24 hours a day, five days a week, with trading sessions corresponding to different time zones around the world. The four main trading sessions are the Sydney session, the Tokyo session, the London session, and the New York session. Each session has its own unique characteristics and trading opportunities, and understanding them can help you make informed trading decisions.

.<h3>The Four Major Trading Sessions. .</h3>
The forex market operates 24 hours a day, but it is divided into four major trading sessions based on the time zones of the major financial centers around the world. The Sydney session starts at 10pm GMT and ends at 7am GMT, the Tokyo session starts at 12am GMT and ends at 9am GMT, the London session starts at 8am GMT and ends at 5pm GMT, and the New York session starts at 1pm GMT and ends at 10pm GMT. Each session has its own unique characteristics and trading opportunities, and understanding them can help you make informed trading decisions.

.<h3>Best Times to Trade Forex. .</h3>
The best times to trade forex are during the overlap of two trading sessions. This is when there is the highest trading volume and volatility, which can lead to more trading opportunities and potential profits. The overlap between the London and New York sessions, from 1pm to 5pm GMT, is typically the most active and liquid time to trade. However, it's important to consider your own trading strategy and preferences when deciding on the best times to trade.

.<h3>Factors Affecting Forex Market Hours.</h3>
The forex market is open 24 hours a day, five days a week, but the trading hours can vary depending on several factors. One of the main factors is the time zone of the country or region where the forex market is located. Another factor is daylight saving time, which can affect the opening and closing times of the forex market. Additionally, holidays and economic events can also impact the trading hours.
