<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l2 level1 lfo2; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">6.<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Keseimbangan Emosi: Keseimbangan emosi

<h3 style="text-align: justify;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Kasus Kesehatan Mental Umum Meliputi:</span></b></h3>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">1.<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Depresi: Depresi adalah gangguan
suasana hati yang ditandai dengan perasaan sedih yang berkepanjangan, hilangnya
minat dalam aktivitas yang biasa dilakukan, kelelahan, gangguan tidur, dan
gangguan konsentrasi.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"><br /></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">2.<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Kecemasan: Kecemasan adalah gangguan
emosional yang menyebabkan perasaan khawatir atau takut yang berlebihan, dan
dapat menyebabkan gejala fisik seperti sakit kepala, sakit perut, atau keringat
dingin.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"><br /></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="line-height: 107%;"><span style="font-family: Adobe Song Std L, serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;">3.</span></span><span style="font-size: xx-small;">&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Gangguan Bipolar: Gangguan bipolar
adalah gangguan suasana hati yang menyebabkan perubahan ekstrem dalam suasana
hati, mulai dari fase mania yang ekstrem hingga fase depresi yang dalam.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"><br /></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">4.<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Skizofrenia: Skizofrenia adalah

perilaku, dan dapat menyebabkan gejala seperti delusi, halusinasi, atau
gangguan bicara.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"><br /></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="line-height: 107%;"><span style="font-family: Adobe Song Std L, serif;"><span style="font-size: 14pt;">5.</span></span><span style="font-size: xx-small;">&nbsp;</span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Gangguan Makan: Gangguan makan, seperti

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">6.<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Ketergantungan: Ketergantungan pada

menyebabkan masalah fisik, emosional, dan sosial yang serius.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"><br /></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;;"><span style="mso-list: Ignore;">7.<span style="font: 7pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></span></span><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD):

kilas balik, dan gangguan emosional lainnya.<o:p></o:p></span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-left: 36pt; mso-list: l0 level1 lfo3; tab-stops: list 36.0pt; text-align: justify; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;"><br /></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: &quot;Adobe Song Std L&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 14pt; line-height: 107%; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;;">Untuk mengatasi kasus kesehatan mental, seseorang mungkin
